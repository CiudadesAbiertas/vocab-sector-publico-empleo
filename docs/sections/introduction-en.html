Introduction back to ToC

The term public employment refers to the employment relationship in which the State is the employer. In all countries the State is the most important employer.

According to the Royal Language Academy, the first meaning of employment is the action of employing.

In this vocabulary we focus in the Public Employment Offer, "a document issued by the Public Administration which sets out the human resources required to carry out the civil service. These job offers are approved by the government of the Public Administration and are published in Official Journals" (campus training).

The Public Administration is a public organisation, the instrument of the government - executive power - to develop and implement public policies and provide services, and acts on the principles of legality, neutrality, effectiveness, efficiency and quality..

The Royal Legislative Degree RDL 5/2015 of 30 October is the legislation by which the consolidated text of the law Ley del Estatuto Básico del Empleado Público (law on the basic status of public employees) is approved.

The public employment announcements are realized through the publication of the Public Employment Offer. After a while of having been published the Public Employment Offer, the public administration publishes the call.

The call gives information of its bases plus a deadline for submission for candidates.

  • Mode of the call: opposition, competition of opposition-competition.
  • Requirements for applying to the call.
  • Number and characteristics of the tests.

The scope of the project has been fixed to achieve the publication of data compatible with the call for public employment search engine due to the fact is a project supported by the General Administration of the State and so it is a good starting point to spread the publication of Open Data with this vocabulary and for further developments.
